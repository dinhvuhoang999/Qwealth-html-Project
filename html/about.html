About Us - QWealth Story and Expert Services

Who We Are

Q Wealth, established in 1996, is a private membership site focused on international asset protection, wealth creation, private offshore banking and the PT lifestyle.

Our expert insights and practical strategies are designed to help you preserve and grow your wealth offshore – and empower you to be financially and personally free.

Q Wealth’s writers and client service staff are digital nomads or Perpetual Travellers who are specialized - and experienced - in internationalization strategies. We are constantly researching the changing political and economic dynamics of the world, and developing opportunities for economic independence, and personal security and freedom.

Our Experts

At Q Wealth, what counts is our service. Personal care and attention are cornerstones of our business. Our job is to give you reliable, trustworthy information – and access to contacts – that you simply wouldn’t find elsewhere. All in a concise, enjoyable and friendly manner.

What We Do

We are not an investment advisory or consultants: we are publishers who provide information that we have rigorously researched and experienced ourselves.

We are interested in identifying the places, sectors and strategies that are part of attractive ecosystems of opportunity. These include: Countries that combine a great lifestyle with paths to second residency and citizenship, as well as attractive economic investment opportunities. Assets and investment opportunities that offer long-term value and security - and which are as shielded as possible from global volatility.

Private banking and offshore incorporation that is designed to complement our members’ unique circumstances and needs. Our areas of focus are not limited to people looking to leave their home countries: the strategies we cover are designed to be relevant to anyone who simply wants to be better prepared for the future in an uncertain world.
